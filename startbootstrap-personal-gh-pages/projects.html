Uni Live

By far my biggest and most ambitious project, UniLive is a social media app just for college students. The idea is to make it easier for students to connect, share, and stay in the loop with what’s happening on their campus. The app will have a global feed for trending posts, a college-specific page for local discussions, and a built-in marketplace for buying and selling stuff like textbooks. I’m starting with University of Cincinnati, but the goal is to expand to colleges everywhere and build a space that actually feels like it belongs to students.
